The family story

Leo's name is still on the sign for a reason.

Leo J. Failoni was born in St. Louis, moved to Kansas City in 1953, and worked for two shoe companies before opening Leo's Pizza in 1974 with his wife, Anna Marie.

The family story around Leo is simple and strong: he loved people, made customers feel welcome, and helped turn a small pizza business into a Northland institution enjoyed across generations.

Today, the restaurant remains connected to the Failoni family, including Leo and Anna Marie's son, Joseph Failoni.
