Installing an Extension

Download the extension folder, place it in Stencyl’s engine-extensions folder, then restart Stencyl. Enable it from your project’s Extensions settings.

Installing a Behavior

Import the behavior into your Stencyl project, attach it to the correct actor or scene, then adjust the exposed settings in the Inspector.
